Scientific Biotech Specialties Inc

Biomed Engineer - Field Service Engineer

2018 - Present:

Responsibilities:

  • Provide technical support to healthcare professionals and laboratory staff to help them use the equipment correctly and troubleshoot minor issues.
  • Train healthcare personnel and laboratory staff on the proper operation and basic maintenance of the equipment.
  • Maintain clear communication with healthcare facility staff and biomedical equipment manufacturers to coordinate service activities, provide updates, and report any issues or concerns.
  • Diagnose and resolve technical issues and malfunction to medical equipment. Repair or replace faulty components to ensure the equipment has proper functionality.
  • Perform routine inspection and maintenance to prevent equipment breakdown and ensure that device is operating at peak efficiency, which would include cleaning, calibrating, and replacing consumables.
  • Install software updates and/ or patches to medical equipment and ensure that all systems are running the latest, secure, and compliant software.
  • Calibrate sensitive medical equipment to maintain accuracy and precision in test results and diagnostic procedures.
  • Maintain detailed service and maintenance records, documenting each service visit, the work performed, and any replacement parts or consumables used.
  • Manage spare parts inventory, ensuring that necessary replacement components are readily available for timely repairs.
  • Ensure that clinic or hospital have reliable and safe equipment.
  • Be available for on-call or emergency service requests to address critical equipment issues outside regular business hours.
